# Bike Sales Visualization Dataset

This Excel file contains summarized sales data for bike products, organized across three worksheets. It provides insights into revenue by **age group**, **product category by country**, and **annual sales and profit trends**. It is intended for practicing data analysis, pivot tables, and visualizations.

## Worksheets

### 1. Revenue by Age Group
- **Columns:** Age_Group, Sum of Revenue
- Shows total revenue contributed by different customer age groups:
  - Youth (<25)
  - Young Adults (25-34)
  - Adults (35-64)
  - Seniors (64+)
- **Grand Total:** Total revenue across all age groups.

### 2. Revenue by Product Category and Country
- **Columns:** Country, Accessories, Bikes, Clothing, Grand Total
- Breaks down revenue by product category for each country:
  - Australia, Canada, France, Germany, United Kingdom, United States
- **Grand Total:** Total revenue by product category and overall.

### 3. Annual Revenue and Profit
- **Columns:** Year, Annual Profit, Annual Revenue
- Shows yearly revenue and profit from 2017 to 2021.
- **Grand Total:** Cumulative revenue and profit over all years.

## Key Insights
- Adults (35-64) and Young Adults (25-34) generate the majority of revenue.
- Bikes contribute the largest portion of revenue compared to Accessories and Clothing.
- Annual revenue and profit show steady growth from 2017 to 2021.
- Useful for pivot tables, charts, and other visualization exercises.

## Usage
This dataset can be used for:
- Data visualization practice
- Sales trend analysis
- Customer demographic analysis
- Product category performance evaluation

## File Information
- **Filename:** `Bike_Sales_Visualization.xlsx`
- **Format:** Excel (.xlsx)
- **Contents:** Three sheets with summarized sales and revenue data.
