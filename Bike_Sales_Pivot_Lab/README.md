
# Bike Sales Dataset - Order Quantity by Country, Age Group, and Gender

This dataset contains summarized sales data showing the total order quantities broken down by **country**, **year**, **age group**, and **customer gender**. It is useful for analyzing customer demographics and sales trends across different regions.

## Dataset Columns

- **Age_Group** – Customer age range:
  - Youth (<25)
  - Young Adults (25-34)
  - Adults (35-64)
- **Customer_Gender** – Gender of the customer (if provided)
- **Country** – Countries included in the dataset (Australia, Canada, France, Germany, United Kingdom, United States)
- **Year** – Year of the sales data (currently only 2021)
- **Order_Quantity** – Total quantity of orders
- **Total columns** – Includes totals by country and grand total across all countries

## Key Insights

- The dataset shows which age groups contribute most to sales in each country.
- Young Adults (25-34) and Adults (35-64) make up the largest portion of orders.
- The **United States** and **Australia** have the highest total orders among the included countries.
- Useful for visualizations such as **pivot tables** and **charts** to compare demographics across regions.

## Usage

This dataset can be used for:

- Sales trend analysis
- Customer segmentation
- Pivot table practice
- Data visualization exercises

## File Information

- **Filename:** `Bike_Sales_Dataset.xlsx`
- **Format:** Excel (.xlsx)
- **Contents:** Full dataset with columns for age group, gender, country, year, and order quantities.
